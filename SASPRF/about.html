About SAS Progressive Research Foundation

SAS Scholars Academic and Scientific Progressive Research Foundation (SASPRF) is a scholarly organization based in Barhawar, Murajhar, Hojai, Assam. Our mission is to support researchers, students, and academicians across disciplines by promoting quality research, organizing academic events, and publishing impactful journals.

Established to nurture innovation and scientific spirit, SASPRF offers membership programs, awards of academic excellence, and collaborations through workshops and conferences.

Greenfort International Publisher

Greenfort International Publisher is the official publishing arm of SASPRF. It oversees the publication of peer-reviewed journals, books, and conference proceedings.

The flagship journal — Greenfort International Journal of Applied Medical Science (GIJAMS) — is an open access, bi-monthly publication available online at www.gipublisher.com.

Our Mission

To build a global network of researchers and scholars who contribute to societal progress through quality education, ethical publishing, and collaborative knowledge exchange.

Our Vision

To be a leading academic and scientific body recognized internationally for empowering educational research and innovation in rural and urban India.
